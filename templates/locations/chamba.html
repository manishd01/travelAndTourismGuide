TOURISM PLACES OF CHAMBA

CHAMBA

chamba,perhaps the loveliest valley of Himachal,is a treasure trove of scenic attractios, meadows,and fertile valleys.The land is populated with a lively people who are endowed with a vibrant culture that stretches back thousands of years.

CHAMERA LAKE

On the route from chamba to Dalhousie,the lake of chamera dam backwaters have developed in to a popular gateway.water sport activities of boating,kayaking,sailing and fun filled escapades.

KHAJJIAR

Khajjiar is a wide grassy clearing around a pond with a thick cedar forest.on higher ground near the pond is the small temple of khajji nag that dates to the 12 th century AD.

BHARMAUR

Bharmaur was known for its grandiose.In the hesrt of Bharmaur town is the historic chaurasi temple complex.Here stand 84 temples and shrines that are dedicated to 84 yogis.

SHAKTI DEVI TEMPLE AT CHHATRARI

shakti devi temple at chhatrari is a well documented temple of chamba -bharmaur built in traditional architecture style.The temple belongs to the period when the first temples of chaurasi at bharmaur were built.

SALOONI

Spread out along a ridge between pir panjal and dhauldhar ranges,salooni, overlooking the bhandal valley is where Bollywood directors come to shoot beautiful sequences.

BHANDAL

Tge bhandal valley opens up at salooni and after passing through langhera goes on to kishtwar in jammu.The valley is rich in flora and fauna and is a popular trekking area.

MANIMAHESH LAKE

Situated close to manimahesh kailash peak in the pir panjal range of Himalayas, manimahesh lake is located in the chamba district and is one of the most important places to visit in chamba.
