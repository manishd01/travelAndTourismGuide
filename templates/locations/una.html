UNA

Una has been identified as a main industrial hub and has become a transit town for travellers going to the city of dharamshala or location with in the himalaya such as kullu ,manali, jawalamukhi , chintpurni. Una is a district the district in the indian state of himachal pardesh . Una shares its border with the hosiarpur and rupanagar district of punjab and kangra , hamirpur and bilaspur district of himachal pardesh .

CHINTPURNI TEMPLE

Chintpurni is a small town in the una district of himachal pradesh about 40 km north of una , not far from the border with the indian state of punjab.

SHEETLA DEVI

sheetla devi is a hindu goddess widely worshiped in the indian subcontinent , notabl in north india. Goddess sheetla devi is worshiped on the eighth day after festival of colours , on the occasion of sheetla asthami.

shiva bari temple is located in the village garget,distt una in himachal pardesh . it is an ancient temple approximately 5000 yrs old. This is also known as drona shiv mandir .
